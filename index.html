Stop Balthazar

Villian Balthazar Bratt has taken over the lab!

Stop him from making more minions!

How to Play

  • A word or sentence will be randomly selected from the word bank
  • Each dash represents a letter- words are separated with spaces
  • Take a guess! Click on the letter you would like to guess
    • If the letter you guess is in the word/sentence, it will appear in the correct spot
    • If you guess wrong- one part of your minion will be built!
    • You can guess incorrectly 7 times before losing the level
  • Scoring:
    • correct letter: +2 points
    • incorrect letter: -1 point
